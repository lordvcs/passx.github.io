Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to PassX ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile picture.
  • Location Data: We collect precise or approximate location information from your mobile device to help you find sports venues and courts near you.
  • Transaction Information: Information about the bookings you make and payments processed through our third-party payment gateway.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the app, including hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To facilitate venue bookings and match organization.
  • To process payments and send transaction confirmations.
  • To communicate with you about updates, promotions, and support.
  • To enhance the security of our application and prevent fraud.

4. Permissions We Request

To provide a full experience, our app may request the following permissions:

  • Location: To find venues near you and provide map-based services.
  • Camera: To scan QR codes for check-ins or venue verification.
  • Storage/Photos: To allow you to upload profile pictures or venue-related images.

5. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • PocketBase: Used as our backend service to store user profiles and booking data securely.
  • Razorpay: Used for processing secure payments. We do not store your credit/debit card details on our servers.
  • Google Maps: Used to provide location-based search and map visualization.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use industry-standard encryption and secure storage (including flutter_secure_storage) to protect your authentication tokens and sensitive information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and as described in our Privacy Policy. If you wish to delete your account or have your data removed from our systems, please contact us at getpassxapp@gmail.com. We will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain data.

8. Your Choices

You can manage your information through the app settings. You may also disable location services through your device settings, though this may limit certain features of the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at getpassxapp@gmail.com.
